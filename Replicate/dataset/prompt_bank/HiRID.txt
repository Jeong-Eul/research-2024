The HiRID database contains all routinely collected data related to patient admissions to the Department of Intensive Care Medicine at the University Hospital of Bern (ICU), Switzerland. Data were extracted from the ICU Patient Data Management System, which is used to prospectively register patient health information, organ function parameter measurements, laboratory test results, and treatment parameters from ICU admission to discharge. HiRID contains: Demographic data, Measurements from bedside monitoring, Measurements and settings of medical devices such as mechanical ventilation, Observations by health care providers e.g.: GCS, RASS, urine and other fluid output, Lab values, Administered drugs, fluids and nutrition.
